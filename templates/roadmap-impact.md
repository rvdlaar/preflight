# Architectuur Roadmap Impactbeoordeling / Architecture Roadmap Impact Assessment

> **Doel / Purpose:** Structured assessment of how a proposed system or change affects the enterprise architecture roadmap. Covers current roadmap position, target architecture alignment, transition architecture, cross-proposal conflicts, and capability gap analysis. Ensures proposals are evaluated in the context of the overall EA direction, not in isolation. Feeds into the PSA and is reviewed by the enterprise architect and EA board.
> **Primaire persona / Primary persona:** Femke (Enterprise Architect / Roadmap-eigenaar)
