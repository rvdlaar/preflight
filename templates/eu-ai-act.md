# EU AI-verordening Risicoclassificatie / EU AI Act Risk Classification

> **Doel / Purpose:** Structured risk classification of AI systems under the EU AI Act (Regulation (EU) 2024/1689). Determines the risk tier, maps applicable obligations, and assesses conformity requirements. In healthcare, clinical decision support systems are almost always classified as high-risk (Annex III, point 5). Feeds into the PSA and is reviewed by the privacy officer, legal counsel, and CMIO.
> **Primaire persona / Primary persona:** Aisha (Informatiearchitect / AI Governance)
