# Operationele Gereedheid Checklist / Operational Readiness Checklist

> **Doel / Purpose:** Structured checklist to verify that all operational prerequisites are in place before a system goes live. Covers monitoring, backup, patching, incident response, DR alignment, access management, and security monitoring. Ensures the infrastructure and operations teams are prepared to run and support the system in production. Feeds into the PSA and is reviewed by the infrastructure lead and CISO.
> **Primaire persona / Primary persona:** Jan (Infrastructuur & Operations)
