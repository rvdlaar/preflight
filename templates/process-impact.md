# Procesimpactbeoordeling / Process Impact Assessment

> **Doel / Purpose:** Structured assessment of how a proposed system or change impacts existing business and clinical processes. Covers as-is and to-be process states, handover analysis, exception paths, process KPIs, and transition planning. Feeds into the PSA and is reviewed by the process architect and relevant domain owners.
